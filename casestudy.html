CASESTUDY ABOUT SPOTIFY

_________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Spotify launched a music-streaming service in late 2008, surpassed 1 million customers in early 2011 . Streaming audio is a competitive business, requiring fast product development, customer understanding, and powerful tools for things like recommendation, music discovery, and connecting people.

Collaboration with Google Cloud:

In 2016, Spotify collaborated with Google Cloud to migrate a large portion of its services and data processing operations to the cloud. This migration involved moving over 1200 online services and 20,000 daily job executions to Google Cloud.

How spotify feel about google cloud ?

“Google Cloud removes a lot of the operational complexity from our ecosystem. That frees up time, We can iterate quicker on key needs, like data insights and machine learning. ”


- Tyson Singer ( VP, Technology & Platform at Spotify)

How big query is important for spotify ?

Spotify's technology leaders point to the particular importance of BigQuery, the Google Cloud data analysis tool, as well as Pub/Sub, for faster software application development. Dataflow, for real-time and historical data analysis, has also been particularly useful.
